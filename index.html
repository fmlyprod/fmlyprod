WHO ARE WE?

Family Productions is a Barcelona-based service production company. We work across Spain for international clients, which is a fancy way of saying: you come with a brief, we make it happen. Simple as that. Mostly. The rest is classified.

The company is technically four years old. The combined experience behind it is not. Marcos has been producing in Spain since before some of our clients were born — 30+ years, former partner at Widescope, the kind of person who's seen every possible disaster on set and fixed it before lunch.

Ketes navigates chaos, contradictory briefs, and last-minute changes like it's a perfectly normal Tuesday. Spoiler: every day is that Tuesday.

We do service production primarily. We're also starting to make our own things — music videos, fashion films, photo shoots — because sitting still was never really an option.

Simple. Transparent. Efficient.
